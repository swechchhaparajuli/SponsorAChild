How does this work ...?


Sponsoring any of these students, means that you will be connected directly to the Sathya Sai Siksha Sadhan school, which you can check out at: ssss.edu.np



Our goal is to find sponsors for all of these students so that each of them are able to receive their education in an environment where the contents of their character will be what defines them rather than any racial, cultural or economic barriers.
